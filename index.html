How to download wallpapers?

Downloading wallpapers is easy! Just follow these simple steps:

  1. Find a wallpaper you like.
    Browse the collection above and choose your favorite.
  2. Click the "Download via Google Drive" button.
    If the button is clickable, a new Google Drive tab will open.
  3. Download the .zip file.
    In Google Drive, click the download icon (down arrow). If prompted about file size, confirm to continue.
  4. Extract the .zip file.
    Open your Downloads folder, right-click (or tap and hold) the .zip file, and choose "Extract".
  5. Set your new wallpaper!
    Open the extracted folder, pick a wallpaper, and set it as your background.

Tip: You need a Google Drive account to download. If you have any issues, please contact us!
